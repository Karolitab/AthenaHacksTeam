itSubscribe

Manage all your subscriptions in one central place!

Start

Our aim is to offer customers a central place to store their subscription information, namely the subscription service, subscription plan, and subscription start date. itSubscribe prides itself on being an easy and simple solution to the problem of forgetting which subscriptions one has and when they started.

Though our services are accessible and useful to all, itSubscribe targets the college student population and younger adult generation, as it is easy to forget which subscriptions he/she/they have agreed to pay for. Especially since the younger adults and college students are still starting their careers, financial management is incredibly important and itSubscribe can help make sure that money is not wasted on unused subscriptions or ending subscriptions. Since the cost of subscriptions can easily add up, itSubscribe enables users to remember their subscriptions and estimate when the next payment or renewal will happen; therefore, this will aid them in making the decision of whether or not they want to continue paying with their plan or cancel their plan.
